## About Hidden Japan Tokyo

Hidden Japan Tokyo is an independent travel guide focused on lesser-known destinations across Japan — the kind of places that don't make the front page of guidebooks but reward travelers who seek them out.

We write primarily for English-speaking visitors to Japan, with guides also available in Korean (한국어) and Traditional Chinese (繁體中文).

We are not affiliated with any tourism board or government organization. Our recommendations are based on personal research and travel experience.
