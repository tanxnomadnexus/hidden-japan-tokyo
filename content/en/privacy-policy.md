## Introduction

Hidden Japan Tokyo ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website hidden-japan.tokyo. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our site, we may automatically collect:

- Browser type and version
- Pages visited and time spent on each page
- Referring website
- General location (country/region level only)

This information is collected through cookies and similar technologies.

### Information You Provide

We do not require registration or account creation. If you contact us via email, we collect only the information you choose to share.

## Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

**Analytics:** We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects anonymized data about page views and user behavior. You can opt out via [Google's opt-out tool](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

**Advertising:** We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show ads based on your interests. You can manage your ad preferences via [Google's Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/).

**Affiliate links:** We participate in the Booking.com affiliate program. Clicking affiliate links may set cookies on your device to track referrals.

## Third-Party Services

Our site uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

- **Google Analytics** — [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Google AdSense** — [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Booking.com Affiliate Program** — [Privacy Policy](https://www.booking.com/content/privacy.html)

## Affiliate Disclosure

Hidden Japan Tokyo participates in affiliate programs including Booking.com. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase or booking, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you. We only recommend properties and services we believe are genuinely useful to travelers.

## Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. Analytics data is retained by Google according to their own policies.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access information collected about you
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of analytics and advertising tracking

To exercise these rights or for any privacy-related questions, please contact us at the email address on our [Contact page](/contact/).

## Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
